Deployment Guide Manual

Optimized for universal deployment across standard subnet boundaries and smart mesh networks (like TP-Link Deco).

Step 1: Discover Nodes Tap Scan Network. Mesh environments will discover all live targets across both wired and wireless bands.
Step 2: Save Target Inventory Look through the temporary discovery table and click Import Node *only* for the specific wired systems you wish to control.
Step 3: Enable Single-Click Control Open the Set Credentials drawer, type your target host account credentials once, and enjoy streamlined one-touch power actions!
